Why Canada is Your Perfect Destination

Canada offers a unique combination of impressive wildlife and landscapes of absolute peace. The Canadian Rockies are home to ancient glaciers, vibrant turquoise lakes, and extraordinary fauna including grizzly bears, moose, bighorn sheep, and eagles.

During this week, you'll experience nature in its purest form, with specialized wildlife tours and moments of absolute tranquility surrounded by the most impressive mountains in North America.

Day three is entirely dedicated to Moraine Lake, located in the Valley of the Ten Peaks. Considered by many as the most beautiful lake in the Canadian Rockies, with a unique deep blue color.

DAY 6

Maligne Lake & Spirit Island

Your sixth day is dedicated to exploring the beautiful Maligne Valley, starting with a stop at Medicine Lake, known as the "disappearing lake" due to its unique underground drainage system.

‚õµ Maligne Lake Cruise to Spirit Island

The day's highlight is the 90-minute cruise to Spirit Island on Maligne Lake. Maligne Lake is the largest natural lake in the Canadian Rockies, stretching 22 km between snow-capped mountains and glaciers.

Spirit Island is one of Canada's most photographed places. A moment of absolute peace, surrounded by pristine nature and silence interrupted only by water and wind.

Wildlife during cruise: Black and grizzly bears on shores, Harlequin ducks, bald eagles fishing, moose swimming in bays.
